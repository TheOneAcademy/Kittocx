Elephant The Barbaric

Brute Force can be countered with Wits

  • Moral: Wrath
  • Weakness: A Calm Mind triumphs a Wrathful One

Elephant once a Destructive Forest Giant, roamed the land bringing ruin to those close to it. The Forest dwellers often were scared for their life as every movement caused a ripple in the air. Many tried to appeal to the Forest Giant to no avail, soon the ground mice began to plan a revolution.

Entering the Giant's ears in its sleep, they infiltrated and reached its eardrums. Beginning its operation it bit deeply into it causing immense pain and suffering to the once Violent Giant. Riddled in pain the Elephant tried to headbutt trees, diving into the river but no good.

Succumbing to the pain, the giant begged for mercy just and dwellers had. Unlike the Wrathful Elephant, the mice released and showed that pain was a fraction of what they had felt. From then on, The Peaceful Giant was born and continues to tread lightly in fear of repeating its sins.
