Slow Loris The Rogue

A moment of weakness makes all the difference

  • Moral: Lust
  • Weakness: Strayed from the original road

Once a well respected Royal Bomoh who healed all. Many animals would travel far and wide seeking the Great Royal Bomoh. Even the King would ask for personally the Royal Bomoh's Pressence on occassion.

A fateful day the princess had nearly drowned from pains in the leg and stomach. Saved by her hand maidens, the Royal Bomoh was rushed to examine her. However upon closer inspection, the princess's beauty bewitched the bomoh and their hand strayed where it should not.

Angered, the King demanded for both eyes be taken as compensation for the transgression. The Royal Court could bear to do such harm to the Bomoh who had healed them prior, hatched a plan. Using a mixture of herbs, they covered Slow Loris's eyes causing the illusion of "Blinding" before exiling them for life.
