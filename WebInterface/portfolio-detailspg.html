Pangolin The Deceitful

Lying will one day come back to bite you

  • Moral: Truth
  • Weakness: Playing Dead can only help you so much

A trickster who used it skills in deception to capture its prey. Sleeping on the forest floor awaiting for the perfect moment to pounce. Satisfied, it enters the depths of the river where its prizes would flow above water.

One Fateful day, a bloodthirsty forest giant emerged hungered beyond measure. Catching the "Dead" Pangolin it tried to smash it against the trunks of mother nature. Surprised it did not let go, Pangolin held tightly beyond understanding or reason.

Days had passed, Elephant exhausted beyond measure finally succumbed to its injuries and passed. Pangolin still fearful did not notice and held on continously without fail. So too did Pangolin fall and the prey surrounded Pangolin and the cycle had changed.
