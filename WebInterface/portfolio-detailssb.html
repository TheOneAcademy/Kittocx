Sun Bear the Heroic

Anyone can become a hero

  • Moral: Courage
  • Weakness: Sacrifice

Once loved by a strong mother, she sacrificed herself to save young Sun Bear. The Tiger lived on as an enigma for the young cub scarring its early life. But one fateful encounter, a family of orang asli took pity and adopted the cub.

Raised in the Village, Sun Bear grew to be loved by all. It would even grow the courage to join on hunts and bring spoils. On a lucky day Sun Bear returned with a boar, shared with the entire village.

Night had dawned, all had returned to the land of slumber. Rustling could be heard and Sun Bear saw the bane of his existence. Battling both forces fell, Sun Bear sacrificing itself from the tiger and showing love triumphs all logic.
