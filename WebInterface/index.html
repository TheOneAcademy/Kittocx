About

Learn of the Author

Author & Malaysian Zoologist

Pioneering the early Bio-Diversity in Malaysia, and writing on stories from his travels around the country.

  • Birthday: 21 August 1926
  • Website: www.example.com
  • Phone: n/a
  • City: Cheras, Kuala Lumpur
  • Age: 93

Dr Lim Boo Liat was a Malaysian Zoologist who helped pave the way for the research and development of the field of Zoology in Malaysia. He wrote in his free time and penned many scientific research, books and in this case a collection of short stories from his travels around the Orang Asli Communities.
